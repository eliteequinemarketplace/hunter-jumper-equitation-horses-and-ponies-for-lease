Short-Term & Seasonal Leases — Show Leases & Circuit Leases

Show leases and circuit leases are increasingly common in the hunter jumper world — short-term arrangements where a rider leases a quality horse for a specific show, a series of shows, or an entire circuit season. Circuit leases on the Florida winter circuit, the fall indoor circuit, and major summer circuits are available on Elite Equine Marketplace from trainers and sellers offering their horses for competitive lease arrangements during peak show seasons.

Catch Rides

A catch ride is a one-time or very short-term riding opportunity on a quality show horse — typically arranged at the professional level for riders between horses, or for amateur riders seeking competitive experience on a horse owned by someone else. Catch rides on quality hunter jumper horses are available on Elite Equine Marketplace from trusted trainers and sellers worldwide.
